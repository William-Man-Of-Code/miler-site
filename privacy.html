Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Miler (“we,” “our,” “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and Profile Data: Basic info such as name, email, and preferences if you choose to create an account.
  • Workout & Device Data: GPS location, pace, distance, and time recorded during runs.
  • Health Data: If you connect Apple Health or similar services, we may process data such as heart rate or step count (only with your permission).
  • Usage Data: Device type, app version, and interaction logs to help improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized training plans and track your progress.
  • To provide real-time pace guidance and performance insights.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App.
  • To communicate with you about updates or support.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA Users)

For users in the European Economic Area, processing is based on your consent, the performance of a contract (providing the App), compliance with legal obligations, or our legitimate interests in improving our services.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We may share anonymized or aggregated data for analytics and performance metrics.
  • We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.
  • We may share data with trusted vendors (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) who process it only on our behalf and under confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored securely in encrypted form on servers located in the United States. We retain data only as long as necessary for legitimate purposes or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time (if processing is based on consent).
  • Portability of data to another service provider.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority (EU/UK residents).

7. Cookies & Analytics

Miler does not use advertising cookies. Limited analytics tools may collect anonymous usage metrics to improve app performance.

8. Children’s Privacy

Miler is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us for deletion.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (HTTPS, AES-256) and access controls to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, but we continually update our safeguards.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate protections consistent with applicable privacy laws (e.g., EU Standard Contractual Clauses).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through the App or our website, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please email support@milerapp.com.
